Blackberry Mousse Tart

Vegan, no-bake, and easy to make!

This delicious vegan blackberry mousse tart is everything you could want in simple dessert!

With a creamy blackberry and blueberry based mousse filling, and a no-bake oreo crust, this tart is sure to be a hit with everyone!

Ingredients

  • 320g oreo cookies
  • 80g melted vegan butter
  • 225g blackberries and blueberries
  • 225g vegan cream cheese
  • 75g sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 240ml chilled coconut cream
  • 60ml vegan milk
  • 1 tsp agar powder

Steps

Crust

  1. Add oreos into blender until finely ground
  2. Mix melted butter with crumbs
  3. Press mixture into the tart pan
  4. Place in freezer for 15 minutes to firm up

Filling

  1. Add berries, cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla to blender, and blend until smooth
  2. Whip coconut cream until stiff peaks form, and refrigerate until ready for use
  3. In a saucepan, mix agar-agar into vegan milk, and bring to a boil until cooked
  4. Fold agar-agar mixture into berry puree mix, then fold in the whipped coconut cream
  5. Pour mixture into tart pan and refrigerate overnight to firm up
  6. Decorate tart with berries, flowers, whipped cream, nuts, or other toppings!
